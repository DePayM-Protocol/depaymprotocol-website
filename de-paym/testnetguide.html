How to use the De-PayM app

Quick start

1. Install the De-PayM app (Play Store or official APK from official website).
2. Register (username, email, password) and verify your email.
3. Connect a wallet (create or import), then tap Start Mining.

1. Access via referral (optional)

• Open a referral link to auto-fill the referral code on the registration page.
• Or paste a referral code you received from a friend in the referral field to boost your mining power.

2. Install the app

• If you registered on the web, download the official De-PayM app from the Google Play Store or obtain the official APK from the De-PayM Protocol website.
• Always use official sources to avoid fake apps.

3. Register

• Enter username, email and a strong password.
• Password rules: at least one uppercase letter (A–Z), at least one number (0–9), and a minimum of 8 characters.

4. Verify your email

• You will receive an 8-digit verification code by email. Enter it in the app to verify your account.
• If you don’t see the email, check Spam/Junk and try resending from the app.

5. Connect a wallet

• Tap Connect Wallet. You can create a new wallet or import one using your Seed/Mnemonic phrase or Private key (EVM wallets supported).
• After success, your wallet is linked to the app.

6. Start mining & boost

• Tap Start Mining.
• To boost your mining power, make on-chain transactions with friends via the app’s Pay and P2P (Withdraw / Deposit) features — create QR codes for friends to scan and transact. On-chain activity increases mining power.

Security — very important

• Never share your Private Key or Seed/Mnemonic phrase. Anyone with them can access your funds.
• The De-PayM team will never ask for these. If anyone asks, it’s a scam.
• Back up your seed phrase offline (written on paper). Do not store it in cloud notes or share it.

Troubleshooting & tips

• No verification email: wait a few minutes, check Spam, or resend.
• Referral not applied: use the referral link during registration or paste the code in the referral field.
• Lost wallet access: import your wallet using your seed/private key. If you do not have them, funds cannot be recovered.

Quick checklist

  • Installed official app
  • Registered with email & strong password
  • Verified email
  • Connected or created EVM wallet (seed backed up)
  • Clicked Start Mining
  • (Optional) Entered referral code to boost mining

Tip: Keep the app updated and only download official updates to keep your funds and account safe.
